Our Turnkey 6-Step Process

From initial DISCOM load feasibility and SLD single-line diagrams to high-voltage field execution and 24/7 CMS uptime monitoring.

1
STAGE 01

Site Survey & Load Feasibility

Certified electrical engineers conduct comprehensive site audits, measuring transformer headroom, cable distance, and earthing resistance.

  • ✓ Transformer capacity & peak load analysis
  • ✓ Earthing pit location & earth resistance test
  • ✓ Digital site layout & cable route mapping
2
STAGE 02

DISCOM Approval & SLD Clearance

Preparation and submission of official CEA-compliant Single Line Diagrams (SLD) and DISCOM sanction load applications.

  • ✓ CEA & State DISCOM load sanctioning
  • ✓ Professional electrical SLD engineering
  • ✓ Dedicated EV tariff meter processing
3
STAGE 03

Civil Works & Cabling Trenching

Heavy-duty civil foundation construction, IS-compliant dual earthing pits, and armored XLPE copper/aluminum cable laying.

  • ✓ Dual chemical earthing pit installation
  • ✓ Armored XLPE high-voltage trenching
  • ✓ Weatherproof IP65 distribution panel setup
4
STAGE 04

Charger Mounting & Protection Sync

Precision mounting of AC Type-2 and DC Fast Chargers with dedicated MCB, RCCB, and surge protection device (SPD) sync.

  • ✓ Dual-gun 60kW-120kW DC fast charger setup
  • ✓ Class B+C Surge Protection Device (SPD)
  • ✓ Automated RCCB trip protection
5
STAGE 05

Load Testing & Commissioning

Full thermal imaging inspection, high-voltage load testing, and final DISCOM meter seal commissioning.

  • ✓ FLIR thermal camera junction check
  • ✓ Simulated full-capacity EV charge cycle
  • ✓ DISCOM inspector sign-off certificate
6
STAGE 06

OCPP CMS & 24/7 SLA Operations

Connecting chargers to smart OCPP 1.6J Central Management Software (CMS) with guaranteed 72-hour field maintenance SLA.

  • ✓ Automated billing, RFID & app integration
  • ✓ 99.9% uptime cloud health telemetry
  • ✓ 72-hour guaranteed on-ground technician SLA
