Installing EV chargers requires a formal Single Line Diagram (SLD) clearance, DISCOM load sanctioning, and dedicated EV tariff meter connection. IGP handles 100% of the DISCOM documentation, application filing, and site inspection across all 28 states.
AC Type-2 chargers (7.4kW to 22kW) supply alternating current directly to the vehicle's onboard charger, ideal for overnight residential or office park charging (4-8 hours). DC Fast Chargers (60kW to 120kW+) bypass the onboard charger, delivering direct current directly to the battery pack for ultra-fast charging (20-40 minutes).
Our standard turnkey execution takes between 5 to 14 days depending on DISCOM load sanction approval timelines. Civil trenching, earthing pit installation, and charger mounting are completed within 72 hours of site clearance.
